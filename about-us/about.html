ABOUT US

Our Story

Established in August 2010, Foodies believes in facilitating it’s guests with an experience of fine dining at reasonable prices and extra ordinary services.

Our chefs are a connoisseur 0f food, be it a fast-tracked lunch or a slow-mover exquisite dinner. From fast food to Chinese, BBQ to seafood, enjoy every bit of our traditional cuisines and experience greatness with our diversified menu.

Relish the great moments and exceptional variety of food with us. Foods Inn also allows you to celebrate your birthdays and special occasions with unmatched services.

Our Speciality

Our special dish is cooked by our chefs that have perfected the technique of cooking so that you can enjoy each of these recipes as if you were eating them fresh, back home. Cooked fresh while you wait, our key dish is known for its fresh (never frozen) ingredients— just like home.

Our barbequed meats are marinated for over 24 hours in our house-special spices then cooked fresh inside a clay tandoor giving it that authentic taste. The barbeque dishes are then served over charcoal giving it our signature Foodies style. Oh and just in case you were wondering, our meat is sourced locally and never frozen.

Our Purpose

To cook with heart and serve upscale Fast food, Chinese, Karahi, kababs & BBQ to couples, coworkers, and families across the GTA in a casual, vibrant, and nostalgic atmosphere.

Our Vision

We will be the leader in Vietnamese restaurant franchising with excellent flavour, organic food offerings, and a consistent experience that revolves around fresh food.

Our Mission

Ho Chi Minh city is still known for its vibrant nightlife, immense sense of culture, and delicious food. We offer a “just like home” menu for Vietnamese and a unique space for Tourists.
